Our story

Two friends,
one dream

De Vriendschap is a business built around an idea. Our idea is that friendship makes the world a little more beautiful.

We are Kamiran Hame and Gert Dijkhuizen. Our friendship makes us happy and strong together. So strong, in fact, that we started a business together. And… friendship is too beautiful not to share with others!

We want to work the same way with our customers. Kindness, trust, attention. That's who we are — it's right there in our name. Because we know that friendship makes the world a little more beautiful.
